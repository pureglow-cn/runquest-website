✦ Our Story

A Legacy of
Exceptional Hospitality

Established in 1998, The Grand Aurum has been the benchmark of luxury hospitality in South Asia. Nestled in a heritage building reimagined for the modern traveler, every corner tells a story of timeless elegance and meticulous craftsmanship.

From the hand-curated art on our walls to the thread count of our linens, no detail is too small. Our award-winning team of over 400 hospitality professionals exists for one purpose — to make your stay extraordinary.

✦ Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Standard check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out are available based on room availability and may be subject to additional charges. Gold and Platinum members enjoy guaranteed early check-in and late check-out.

Most room rates include a complimentary breakfast for two at our flagship restaurant. Presidential Suites and Garden Villas include all meals, beverages, and in-room dining at no additional charge. Please check your booking confirmation for specific inclusions.

Yes, we offer complimentary luxury airport transfers for all reservations of 2 nights or more. Our chauffeur-driven Rolls-Royce Phantom or Mercedes S-Class will be waiting for you at the arrivals terminal. Please inform us of your flight details at least 24 hours in advance.

Reservations can be cancelled without charge up to 48 hours before arrival. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a one-night charge. Non-refundable rate bookings are not eligible for cancellation. Presidential Suites and Villas have a 7-day cancellation policy.

We welcome well-behaved pets (under 10 kg) in our Garden Villas. A dedicated pet concierge service is available including gourmet pet meals, grooming, and a custom pet bed and amenity kit. A refundable pet security deposit of ₹5,000 applies. Please inform us prior to arrival.

Our business centre features 8 private meeting rooms, a 250-seat conference hall, and state-of-the-art AV equipment. Dedicated business concierge, high-speed fibre internet, printing and secretarial services are available 24/7. Corporate accounts are available on request.
